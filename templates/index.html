Steps to use the application:
  1. Upload Chest X-Ray Image below. The image should be either in JPG or JPEG or PNG format. Do not use grayscaled images (The uploaded image must have 3 color channels for the prediction to work). Also, only one image can be uploaded for processing.
  2. Click on Upload & Get Prediction button.
  3. Wait for the server to process the image and give the prediction results. (This might take a few seconds or a couple of minutes)

You can use these sample images for testing (download and upload them): Normal Chest X-Ray Pneumonia Chest X-Ray

Accepted Image Formats: JPG, JPEG & PNG
